To run snake_final.py, simply call 'python snake_final.py' in the folder containing the file.

To play, use the arrow keys to control the snake head. Collect the multi-colored food to grow your tail. Do not let the head run into its own tail or the edges of the screen, or you lose. Press enter to restart the game, or press escape to exit the game at any time.
